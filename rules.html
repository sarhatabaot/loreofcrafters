You are responsible for your own account. Anything that is done on your account will be seen as done by you, and will be treated as such. Please do not message any staff member with the excuse that someone else used your account. There is no way to verify this, and as such, it will not be tolerated as an excuse.

If you are banned under one account, you may not play on other accounts on the site while the ban is still in effect. If you're caught playing on alt accounts while your main account is banned will result in a permanent ban of your main & alt account.

Community Rules (Applies everywhere):

  • Use common sense
  • No griefing, including but not limited to:
    • Destroying blocks placed by others.
    • Placing blocks or leaving in inappropriate signs on near other players structures.
    • Flooding structures.
    • Spamming items.
    • Destroying crops without replanting.
    • WorldEdit griefing.
  • No 'grief-baiting'. This means that players may not try to get other players in trouble by tricking/forcing them to destroy structures in an attempt to get the player kicked, banned or warned.
  • No sexism, racism, homophobia or any type of hate speech, especially targeted at specific users.
  • Have respect for all users. Harassment of the staff, or anyone else can result in a kick or ban.
  • Players using modifications to change gameplay may be kicked, or banned without warning depending on the severity to the situation. This includes but is not limited to:
    • NoClip
    • Invincibility
    • Speed modification
    • Xray / ore-detection
    • Duping hacks
  • No attempts to circumvent any protections on the server, including but not limited to:
    • The server script
    • WorldGuard
      • Attempts to do so without permissions will result in a ban.
  • Invisible or glitched players' data may be reset without warning.
  • No sharing of illegal content.
  • No trolling in chat.
  • No posting of other people's personal information without their explicit permissions in general chat or to anyone else on the servers, including but not limited to:
    • Names
    • Addresses
  • No offensive or inappropriate skins. Failure to change when asked by a mod will result in a ban until its changed.
  • No impersonation of staff members.
  • No advertising or non LoC branded servers or content.
  • Do not ask for IP’s, domains or website addresses of other servers or content.
  • No extreme use of CAPS.
    • 2 or more sentences of CAPS in a row will get you kicked or muted.

 

           The LoC administrative team has the privilege to modify, remove or add any rule that may be needed for future circumstances.
